The Shock of the Natural; Works in Brooklyn Have a Long Tradition

Oct. 5, 1999


Call them shocking, schlocky, ''sick stuff,'' profane, inane, puerile, gross, engrossing, brave, self-indulgent, profound or not worth the fluid they're pickled in, but the most ferociously debated art objects from the hand-wringing ''Sensation'' show at the Brooklyn Museum of Art have a long and hoary history behind them.
